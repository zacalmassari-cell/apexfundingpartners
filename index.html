What you could be claiming
Boiler Upgrade Scheme
Up to £7,500

Replace your gas boiler with a heat pump and the government covers a significant portion of the cost. Available to homeowners across England and Wales.

Great British Insulation Scheme
Free insulation installation

Loft, cavity wall, and solid wall insulation fully funded for eligible homeowners. Eligibility is broader than most people expect — income thresholds have been relaxed.

Disabled Facilities Grant
Up to £30,000

Local council funding for home adaptations — stairlifts, accessible bathrooms, widened doorways. Available to homeowners and renters. The application process is notoriously difficult. We handle it entirely.

ECO4 Scheme
Free home energy upgrades

Heating systems, insulation, and energy measures fully funded through energy supplier obligations. Over 4 million homes are eligible. The referral and assessment chain is complex — we navigate it for you.

Why most people never claim
The paperwork

Each scheme has its own application portal, eligibility criteria, supporting documents, and approval chain. Most people start and abandon within an hour.

The jargon

Government grant schemes are written by civil servants for civil servants. EPC ratings, primary heat sources, means testing, and means-tested benefit triggers — it's deliberately impenetrable.

The time

A single grant application takes 4–8 hours minimum if you know what you're doing. Most homeowners don't — so it takes days of back-and-forth before they give up entirely.
